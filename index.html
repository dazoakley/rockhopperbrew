About Us

We're an independent brewery based in Luton, Bedfordshire. It’s the result of a passion for homebrewing, and beer in general that very happily got a bit out of hand.

We will be brewing beer that is unpasteurised, unfiltered and packed to the brim with flavour – this is the kind of beer we love, and we hope you do too.

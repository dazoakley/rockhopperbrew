We're home brewers at heart, and like experimenting and trying new things.

This is reflected in our beer line up - a few core styles, but with variations of each showcasing different ingredients and different approaches to the style.

{% img 'beers/pale.png' class:'img-responsive center-block beer-pump-label' alt:'PALE' %}

PALE
our take on modern pale ales

Brewed using a common malt base, but with a different and seasonal hop combinations you'll find a different take on the pale ale style with each variation.

Variations

  • 01 | NZ Cascade, Dr. Rudi - Using all NZ hops, you'll find flavours and aromas of grapefruit, lemongrass and other citrus notes and a gentle bitterness backing it all up.
  • 02 | Target, NZ Cascade - Using a a combination of UK and NZ hops, Pale | 02 gives you subtle grapefruit and citrus flavours combined with the gentle spicy bitterness expected from traditional British hops.

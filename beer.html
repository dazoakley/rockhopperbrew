We're home brewers at heart, and like experimenting and trying new things.

This is reflected in our beer line up - a few core styles, but with variations of each showcasing different ingredients and different approaches to the style.

PALE
our take on modern pale ales...

Brewed using a consistent malt base, but with a different and seasonal hop combination you'll find a different take on the pale ale style with each variation.

Variations

  • 01 | NZ Cascade & Dr. Rudi - Using all NZ hops, you'll find flavours and aromas of grapefruit, lemongrass and other citrus notes and a gentle bitterness backing it all up.
